Planting Carrots

Today we're going to take you through the process of planting carrots.
Some people are often nervous to plant carrots in their vege-gardens, however, by following this guide you'll quickly see that it's no more difficult than onions.
Alright, let's get started.
Firstly you're going to need your carrot seeds. If you're planting carrots for the first time you may want to go with rolly carrots. Don't be alarmed when these come out round, they're meant to.
